Welcome to the AI Accountability and Explainability tool

This is an experimental website for my diploma thesis with title: Research and Development of an evaluation system for Machine Learning, Explainability and Accountability methods, part of 5-years undergraduate studies at the Computer Enginering and Informatics Department at University of Patras.

→The experimental website consists of two major parts. The first one focuses on defining an algorithmic system's Accountability from an Organizational and Algorithmic perspective. We provide a questionnaire that must be completed, in order to define the percentage of your system's Accountability.

→The seconds part focuses on Explainability. We propose an experimental platform with three different models that make predictions on completely different fields and then we explain how the prediction was made with the help of SHAP. This will stand as an example for future organizations or institutes, by establishing the fact that if a prediction is coming from an artificial intelligence model ,then it is imperative to explain thoroughly to the end-users how it was made . We hope that in the future, all the predictions coming from an AI model, can be explained, something that today is far from the truth.
